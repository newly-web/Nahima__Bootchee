Who is Bootchee

Born in Haiti and raised in Canada, Nahima Bootchee, professionally known as Bootchee, is a singer-songwriter blending R&B, Pop and Ballad with emotional depth and melodic storytelling. Inspired by iconic artists like Toni Braxton, Mariah Carey, Rihanna, Leona Lewis, Adele and Lana Del Rey, Bootchee creates songs that resonate with today’s generation while reflecting an old soul’s wisdom.

Music has always been a part of her life. Growing up in a musical home, she cultivated a keen musical ear and a passion for storytelling through song. At 16, she officially stepped into the music scene by sharing covers on YouTube and performing at local events, earning recognition for her soft yet piercing soprano vocals that convey both vulnerability and strength. As a singer-songwriter, every lyric she writes and every note she sings is deeply personal, offering listeners an authentic glimpse into her world. Her sound is both timeless and fresh, speaking to hearts ready to feel and souls longing to connect. Stay tuned as Bootchee is making her mark in the music scene.
